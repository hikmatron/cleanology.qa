Some frequently asked questions
Why should I choose Cleanology Qatar for cleaning services?

Cleanology Qatar is an ISO- and BICSc-certified company providing specialized, science-based cleaning solutions. We focus on quality, safety, and eco-friendly service across homes and businesses.

Do you handle both residential and commercial cleaning?

Yes, Cleanology offers cleaning for villas, apartments, offices, retail stores, restaurants, and industrial sites ensuring every project meets international hygiene standards.

Are your cleaning products safe and eco-friendly?

Absolutely. We only use certified, non-toxic, and biodegradable products that are safe for families, pets, and the environment while delivering effective results.

How often should I schedule professional cleaning?

We recommend deep cleaning every 6–12 months and HVAC duct cleaning every 1–2 years to maintain a healthy, dust-free indoor environment.

What specialized cleaning services do you offer?

Our services include water tank cleaning, HVAC duct cleaning, sofa and upholstery cleaning, post-construction cleanup, façade cleaning, and industrial shutdown cleaning.

Which areas in Qatar does Cleanology cover?

We serve clients across Doha, Lusail, The Pearl, West Bay, Al Rayyan, Al Wakrah, and all major regions of Qatar — with flexible scheduling and fast response.

How does Cleanology ensure consistent quality?

Each job is supervised by trained professionals, documented with before-and-after photos, and completed under ISO 9001 quality management procedures.

Can I book maid or housekeeping services easily?

Yes! Through our Qatar Maids platform available on Android and iOS or via www.qatarmaids.qa

Is your service available 24/7 in Qatar?

Yes. Cleanology operates round-the-clock with emergency response and flexible scheduling to suit your needs — even on weekends and holidays.
