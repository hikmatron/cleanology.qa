Certified Water Tank Cleaning & Disinfection Services Across Qatar

Benefits of Water Tank Cleaning

Why Water Tank Cleaning Is Essential?

Protect Health & Hygiene

Tanks accumulate sediment, algae, rust, and biofilm — leading to waterborne diseases and contamination risks.

Maintain Water Quality

Avoid discoloration, odor, and taste issues caused by stagnant or dirty tanks.

Extend System Lifespan

Regular cleaning prevents corrosion, damage to pipes, valves, and internal surfaces.

Prevent Clogs & Flow Issues

Sludge and debris can block outlets, filters, and pumps, disrupting water supply.

Ensure Compliance with Qatar Standards

Stay aligned with municipal health codes for clean, safe water storage.

The Best Way to Do

Our Structured Water Tank Cleaning Process

Inspection & Dewatering

We assess tank material (GRP, plastic, metal, concrete), sediment levels, biofilm presence, and access points. Tanks are then safely emptied using approved pumps, with water stored or disposed of per local regulations.

Sediment & Sludge Removal

Sand, silt, debris, and settled waste are removed using a combination of mechanical and manual tools.

Internal Scrubbing & Jet Washing

Walls, floors, joints, and corners are scrubbed with non-abrasive, material-safe tools. Controlled high-pressure jets eliminate stubborn residue and grime from internal surfaces.

Vacuum Extraction & Safe Disposal

Contaminants are extracted using industrial wet vacs or filtered collection systems, ensuring safe and compliant disposal.

Certified Disinfection & Drying

We apply potable-water-grade disinfectants, UV, or chemical treatments to eliminate bacteria, viruses, and algae. If needed, the tank is dried and ventilated to ensure safe conditions before refill.

Final Inspection & Refilling

A supervisor conducts quality control checks, provides before/after documentation, and refills the tank with clean water.

Some frequently asked questions

Water Tank Cleaning FAQs

How often should water tanks be cleaned in Qatar?

At least every 3–6 months for domestic tanks. More often in high-use or sensitive settings.

Will cleaning affect my water supply?

Briefly — water is shut off only during draining and refilling phases. You can remain in the building

Are your cleaning chemicals safe?

Yes — we use certified potable-water-safe disinfectants, fully safe for post-clean use.

How long does it take?

Small tanks take a few hours. Larger or industrial tanks may require half to a full day.
